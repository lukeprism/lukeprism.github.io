About RETRORAID Bingo

This is a bingo board for our lovely little group of memers.

Each square can (and probably will be) completed in any raid where such a goal is possible. I've tried to keep the highly situational tiles to a minimum, but it's no fun if every card is super easy, right?!

RULES:

  • - bets will be pooled into Umbra's FC chest (but of course our subs and non-gobbies can join!!)
  • - for every raid session, Aeo will determine who wants to bet and then select 1 person at random to set the starting bet
  • - all other bets must be between 50% and 100% of the original bet (min 50k, max 1mil) [can be adjusted]
  • - no cheating ofc, we can post our bingos pre-raid :eyes:
  • - the pool is split among all winners
  • - if there are no winners, the pool will be added to the next session
